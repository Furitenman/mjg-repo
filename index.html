Where to Play?

Mahjong Soul (Android / iOS) - English client. Tutorial Included!

  • Majsoul+ - (unofficial) client, available in windows, mac and gnu+linux. Allows you to mod almost everything, even characters.
  • An Introduction to Mahjong Soul (free 2 play guide) - answers most questions about the gacha side of majsoul

Tenhou (Android / iOS) more competitive mahjong

  • Firefox / Chrome - add-ons to translate the interface to English
  • The Tenhou article on the Arcturus wiki - answers most questions about the rules and ranking system

Hime Mahjong (Android / iOS) - better gacha rates and more cosmetics, but no English translations

  • Hime Mahjong Guide - will help you get through the moonrunes enough to play mahjong

Gamedesign - Also known as "that flash game." Best place to practice for beginners if you don't want majsoul's AI. Very simple. Has a list of Yaku on the page as well. Singlehandedly keeping flash alive.
